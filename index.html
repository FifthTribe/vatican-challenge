Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is this year’s specific challenge?

    This year’s challenge is based on the concept of “Care for our Common Home.” We’re looking for solutions and products around climate change and human migration.
    Our Earth is the one home we all share and the only artifact of our common ancestry. Recent changes in climate, however, have forced factions of humanity to migrate. Voluntary migration is hopeful: we are seeking a new home. But forced migration is despairing: we are losing a home. As we show compassion in caring for our home and our Earth, so we should show compassion in caring for those who have lost their homes.

  2. When can I apply?

    Application is open from May 5th to June 5th. Apply here.

  3. Must I accept investment into my company to participate?

    We encourage that, but the answer is: no. Part of our mission is to cultivate the best innovations that address climate and migration. We seek to provide strategic, patient and “compassionate capital” and mentorship, and this is something every team should want to grow. But, you may elect not to participate in the equity investment portion of the challenge.
