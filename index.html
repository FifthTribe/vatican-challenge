A glimpse of the journey

Application Opens

Companies with an innovative solution are asked to apply for a chance to obtain $1M+ in investment and mentorship. Startups must be a registered legal entity to apply. Application Deadline: June 30,2017

+Download 2017 challenge document

Mentor

50 startups will be screened and selected for initial investment and be paired with a dedicated mentor. Mentors will work with startups as needed and provide support to help define your market strategy and scalability. Mentors will advocate on behalf of their startups to the final selection committee for the accelerator.

Accelerator

12 startups, known as the Vatican 12 will be selected to join the 3 month accelerator program in Rome, Italy and receive an additional investment round. Startups will spend time with industry leading mentors, coaches and organizations to refine their business model and market strategy. Upon completion of the Accelerator startups will pitch to a selection committee and a final 6 will proceed to the Showcase round.

Demo Day

The six finalist known as the Vatican 6 will have an opportunity to pitch their solutions to industry leaders for a chance to receive up to $1M in funding to launch and scale their startups. Judges will evaluate each business to determine which are the most transformative, impactful, scalable, sustainable, feasible and profitable.

Who's with you

The Laudato Si Startup Challenge is supported with a global network of companies, experts in leadership, business and environmental stewardship evaluate to help each business be impactful, scalable, sustainable, feasible and profitable.
COMPANIES

Companies will provide participating startups expert advice and support on their business strategy, communications and marketing strategy. Top companies from silicon valley will be supporting the Startups.

MENTORS & JUDGES

50+ mentors and judges will be working directly with startups. Mentors will come from silicon valley and all over the world. They will support teams in any way to help their success in the challenge and connect them with their entrepreneurial eco-system
